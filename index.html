Meal Calories & Macros

Diet Chart

Morning (Empty Stomach)

  • 1 glass warm water
  • Optional: lemon (½)

Breakfast

  • 2 whole eggs (boiled or omelette)
  • 2 slices whole wheat bread
  • 1 glass milk (200 ml)
  • 1 apple or banana

Lunch

  • 2 slices bread OR 1 cup cooked rice
  • 150 g chicken / fish OR 3 eggs
  • Mixed vegetables (1 cup)
  • 1 cup yogurt / curd

Evening Snack

  • 1 fruit (apple / orange)
  • Handful nuts (10–12 peanuts or almonds)
  • Tea or coffee (no sugar preferred)

Dinner

  • 150 g protein (chicken / fish / eggs)
  • Vegetables (1–2 cups)
  • 1 slice bread OR small rice portion

Before Bed (Optional)

  • 1 glass warm milk

Note: Quantity can be increased for muscle gain and reduced for fat loss.
